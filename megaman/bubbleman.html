BUBBLE MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man

To beat Bubble Man in Mega Man 2, use the Metal Blade as your primary weapon, which causes high damage and shreds his shield. The Quick Boomerang or Crash Bomber are also effective. Maintain safe positioning, avoid the ceiling spikes, and jump over his projectiles.

Bubble Man (DWN-011) is one of the eight aquatic Robot Masters created by Dr. Wily in Mega Man 2. He serves as the boss of his own underwater-themed stage and is weak to the Metal Blade. Known for his scuba-diver appearance, he is famously clumsy on land but moves rapidly in his underwater arena.
