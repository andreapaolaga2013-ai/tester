HEAT MAN

To beat Heat Man in Mega Man 2, use the Bubble Lead (Bubble Man's weapon) to cause high damage and stunlock him. Heat Man is predictable: he shoots fireballs, then charges as a flame. Jump over his flame charge, shoot him with Bubble Lead, and repeat until he is defeated.

Heat Man is a fire-themed Robot Master from Mega Man 2 (DWN-012), created by Dr. Wily based on Fire Man's design. Modeled after a Zippo lighter, he is highly heat-resistant, capable of reaching 12,000°C, and uses the chargeable "Atomic Fire" weapon. He is known for being laid-back, liking barbeques, and disliking ice cream.
