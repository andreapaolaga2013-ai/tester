To beat Wood Man in Mega Man 2, use the Metal Blade (Metal Man's weapon), which kills him in a few hits. Stay near the center, jump over his Leaf Shield when he throws it, and attack immediately after. Alternatively, a fully charged Atomic Fire (Heat Man's weapon) deals massive damage

Wood Man (DWN-016) is a boss (Robot Master) in Mega Man 2 created by Dr. Wily, known for being constructed from durable Hinoki cypress wood. He guards a forest stage and attacks using a rotating Leaf Shield and falling leaves. His main weakness is fire-based weaponry, particularly Atomic Fire.
