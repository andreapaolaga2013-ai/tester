To beat Metal Man in Mega Man 2, use the Metal Blade (if re-fighting) or Quick Boomerang to destroy him in just a few hits. If using the Buster, jump over his blades, avoid getting too close, and shoot while moving on the shifting conveyor belt

Metal Man is a boss (Robot Master) and his associated stage in the 1988 NES game Mega Man 2. Widely considered one of the easiest bosses, he is often chosen first, located in a stage featuring conveyor belts and industrial hazards. Defeating him grants the player the powerful Metal Blade weapon.
