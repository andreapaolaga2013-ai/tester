To defeat Crash Man in Mega Man 2, use the Air Shooter (Air Man's weapon) as your primary tactic, which defeats him in 2–3 hits. The most efficient method is to walk toward him, wait for him to jump, and shoot the Air Shooter, which hits him while he is in the air.

Crash Man (or Clash Man in Japan) is a combat Robot Master from Mega Man 2 (1988), designated DWN-013, designed by Dr. Wily. Known as "The Destroyer," he possesses thick armor and specializes in demolition, using his primary weapon, the Crash Bomber—a sticky, drill-type timed explosive. He is known for violent mood swings and high agility.
